Debugging KDE applications with gdb

on 24 Jan 2009
  • Older
  • Newer
A pretty long time has passed since my last post here, and in the meantime I have jumped right into KDE development myself. I have stumbled upon quite a few tricks and pitfalls that I will undoubtedly write about here some day. For now, I just want to share a little gem which I have discovered a few minutes ago: In [@kdesdk/scripts@], David Faure published a little script called [@kde-devel-gdb@] ("view with WebSVN":http://websvn.kde.org/trunk/KDE/kdesdk/scripts/kde-devel-gdb?view=markup), which extends gdb with the ability to print the contents of several Qt containers, including the widely used [@QString@]. Highly recommended!
